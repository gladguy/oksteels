Our Story

Greetings from OK Steels

OK Steels is a leading supplier and distributor of a wide range of steel products, catering to diverse industrial and construction needs. As an authorised dealer of SAIL (Steel Authority of India Limited), we uphold the highest standards of quality and reliability.

We represent ourselves as a reputed firm in steel industries for supplying Iron & Steel, Metals & Alloys, and other construction products in Tamil Nadu. Our dedicated team at our Head Office in Trichy provides prompt service to your organisation.

At OK Steels, we are committed to providing top‑notch steel products that meet stringent quality standards, ensuring reliability and performance for all your structural and industrial needs.
